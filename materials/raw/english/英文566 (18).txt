Registered Mail 
Postal Service Clerk: What can I do for you today? 
Danny: I want to mail this letter to the US. Would 
you please weigh this letter to see what the 
postage is? 
Postal Service Clerk: Sure. Do you want to send it by ordinary or 
registered mail? 
Danny: By ordinary airmail, please. And how long 
will it take to get there? 
Postal Service Clerk: It will take about 1 week. Is there anything 
valuable in it? 
Danny: Yes. There's a postal order for NT$500 
dollars in it. 
Postal Service Clerk: In that case, you'd better have it registered if you want it to reach the desired destination safely. 
Danny: But how will I know when my registered 
mail is received? 
Postal Service Clerk: You can use track & trace service on iPost to find out whether your mail, parcel or 
international mail has been signed for and delivered safely. 
Danny: All right, I'll have it registered then.

Sending a Parcel

Karen: Good morning. I would like to send this parcel to 
Thailand, please. 
Postal Service Clerk: OK, pass me the parcel through the opened window and let's see how much it weighs. It weighs 3 kilos. You need to send it by airmail, it will get there in about 7 days. You also have the option of using international express mail service. 
Karen: What is that? 
Postal Service Clerk: Same as airmail with a faster cross-border postal service, but recipients in Thailand have to sign for 
it when the postman delivers it to them. 
Karen: How much does that cost? 
Postal Service Clerk: The freight will vary based on the weight of the packages or mails. It will be NT$700 if you send it by international EMS. 
Karen: 
Alright. 7 days by airmail is fine. 
Postal Service Clerk: OK, the postage will then be NT$380. Here is your 
change, and please wait for the receipt. Do you 
need anything else? 
Karen: Oh, I need to buy a stamp album and 2 envelopes.

Sally: Also, cash carries lots of bacteria, both on paper 
currency and coins.

drawbacks of mobile payments? 
Sally: Yes. People may worry the cyber security if their smartphone was lost or stolen. Besides, mobile payments might not work properly due to poor network connectivity. However, I still believe that it offers a fast and secure ways to pay for goods.
