01 介系詞 
1. 用途 
(1)表示方位:如at, in, on, above, along, around, before, behind, 
between 等 
15 Don't stand behind the bus. 
別站在公車後面。 
Kevin sits between Jason and John. 
凱文坐在傑森和約翰的中間。 
15 There is a park near my home. 
我家附近有一座公園。 
15 What are they doing under the tree? 
他們在樹下做什麼? 
(2)表示時間:如at, in, on, after, before, between, during, for, 
from, since 
We had a party on January 15th. 
我們在一月十五日舉辦了派對。 
15 The man in back of me was talking during the movie. 
坐在我後方的男子看電影時一直在說話。 
15 We'll take a trip to America in June. 
我們將在六月時去美國旅行。
