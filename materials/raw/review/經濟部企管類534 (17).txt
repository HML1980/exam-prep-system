四、閱讀測驗 
Today, when we want new clothes, we can go to a clothing store and buy whatever you like. But for people two or three hundred years ago, getting new clothes was not so easy. Not only did they make their own clothing, but sometimes people wove their own cloth at home. And if they owned their own sheep, they even spun the wool into yarn themselves. 
Wool cannot just be taken from the sheep and spun. First, it must be cleaned and then carded. In a large family, carding was usually the children's 
106-5 
job because it is easy but tiresome. Carding is done with two wide combs that have rows of steel teeth. The person carding puts a piece of wool on one comb, then pulls the other comb across the wool again and again. This combing makes the wool fibers straight and smooth so they can be spun. 
Finally, the mother or older sister of the family could use the wool for spinning. Spinning just means twisting and stretching the fibers into yarn. You have spun yarn if you have ever pulled and twisted a cotton ball into a long string. Usually, though, a spinning wheel helps make the pulling and twisting easier. The spinner holds the wool in her hands and twists one end onto the yarn already on the spindle, or long winding stick. The spinning wheel turns the spindle, pulling the wool into yarn and winding it onto the spindle at the same time. Finally, the yarn is ready for weaving. 
Today, machines can spin yarn much faster than people can. But in some places, people still make their own yarn because they love the old art of spinning.

(A)水平多角化:又稱橫向多角化。企業以技術上不相關的新產品進入原市場,

(B)策略聯盟:二個以上的企業組織為了特定的商業目的,共同分享自身的資源 
及相關知識的一種合作關係。 
(C)集中策略:集中化策略是指:「將生產資源集中在提供滿足特定買家的需求 上」。集中化策略的表現形式是顧客導向,為特定客戶提供更有效和更滿意 的服務。集中化策略類似於差異化策略,但集中化策略是以滿足特定顧客的 需求為出發點,而差異化則是提供獨特的商品或服務給消費者。 
(D)全球化布局:將公司之事業版圖擴展到母國以外的國家或地區,以使公司能 
在以全球為市場的競爭優勢中脫穎而出的布局方式。 
服務的四大特性: 
(1)無形性:看不見摸不著是服務的特性。因此,競爭的優勢常建立在如何讓無 
形的東西有形化(管理有形環境與降低顧客知覺風險)。 
(2)不可分割性:服務的生產及消費常常同時發生,進行,在企業與消費者互動 
中完成。 
(3)異質性:指服務提供者、場地、時間不同而難以一致。服務業者可採用對 
人員及服務流程的品質管制或標準化來改善此特性的影響。 
(4)易逝性:也稱「不可儲存性」,指服務無法被保存的特性。服務業者為克服 此特性,常採用尖峰、離峰時段差別訂價,或使用預約來管理需求,或應用 營收管理技巧、離峰促銷等。
